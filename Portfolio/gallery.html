Zara Remake

After doing some research and interviews about what people consider to be irritating when it comes to their internet usage many answers pointed to online shopping-related issues. When looking closer at this frustration whit shopping it became clear that it regarded poorly facilitated websites with indifferent navigation. Zara was a recurring name when we asked for an example on such a website.
