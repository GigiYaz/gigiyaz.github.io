Studying at EC-utbildning - Working on my portfolio

I wanted to combine the best of being creative and solving problems. After searching for these two components UX design became the answer!
Where there is human interaction there is UX! The fact that interaction occurs between real human users and services every day was the biggest selling point for me when choosing this career path. All types of products and services from websites, applications to dishwashers hold the focus of UX design.
UX is a parallel between this interaction and this is exactly the work area I want to place myself in.
